Deal Analyzer

A prototype by Collie

Deal Analyzer reconstructs the original investment thesis from an acquisition underwriting model and compares it with actual operating performance. It is designed to help real estate investment professionals quickly understand an investment, identify what has changed since acquisition, and establish context for further analysis.

Your files stay yours. We do not store the underwriting files you upload — they're used only to generate your analysis, then discarded.
